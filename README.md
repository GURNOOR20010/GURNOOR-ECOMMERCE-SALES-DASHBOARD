📊 **Gurnoor Ecommerce Sales Dashboard**
🚀 Project Overview

The Gurnoor Ecommerce Sales Dashboard is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze and visualize ecommerce sales performance. It helps users monitor revenue, profit, order quantity, customer behavior, product performance, and payment trends through dynamic visualizations and filters.

The dashboard enables business owners and analysts to make data-driven decisions by identifying profitable categories, top customers, high-performing states, and monthly profit trends.

🎯 Key Objectives
Track overall sales performance
Analyze profit trends across months
Identify top-performing states
Understand customer purchasing behavior
Evaluate category and sub-category performance
Monitor payment mode preferences

Customer Analysis
Farah was the top customer by purchase amount.
Priyanka and Vrinda showed similar purchasing patterns.
Payment Analysis
COD accounted for 44% of total transactions.
UPI was the second most preferred payment method.
Profit Analysis
April generated the highest profit.
May showed a negative profit trend.
Tables and Printers were the most profitable sub-categories.
